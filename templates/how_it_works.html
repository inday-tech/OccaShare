How OccaServe Works

Booking world-class catering for your event has never been simpler. We connect you with vetted professionals in three easy steps.

Your Journey to a Perfect Event

From discovery to celebration — here's exactly how OccaServe builds unforgettable experiences.

01
Step One

Discover the Perfect Caterer

Browse our curated marketplace of verified professional caterers. Filter by event type — whether it's a grand wedding, intimate birthday, or large corporate gala. Read reviews, explore menus, and compare packages side by side.

  • Filter by event type, location & budget
  • Read verified customer reviews
  • View detailed caterer profiles & menus
02
Step Two

Customize Your Experience

Choose from a variety of curated packages or request a fully custom quote. Tailor the menu to your guests' dietary preferences, cultural traditions, and taste. Discuss your vision directly with the caterer through our messaging platform.

  • Choose from pre-built packages
  • Request custom menus & quotes
  • Communicate directly with caterers
03
Step Three

Book & Relax

Secure your booking with our safe and seamless payment process. Once confirmed, sit back and let our professional caterers handle everything — from setup to service to cleanup. Your only job is to enjoy the moment.

  • Secure online payment via PayMongo
  • Booking confirmation & reminders
  • Full professional setup & service
