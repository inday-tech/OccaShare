Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the OccaServe ecosystem.

How long does verification take for a caterer?
Once all documents are submitted, our team usually completes verification within 3-5 business days. This includes a review of your permits and culinary portfolio.
Is my payment secure?
Yes, we use PayMongo, a bank-grade payment gateway. Your sensitive information is never stored on our servers, and every transaction is encrypted.
Can I cancel a booking after paying the downpayment?
Cancellations are subject to each caterer's specific policy. You can find these details in the "Terms & Conditions" section of your individual booking.
How do I request a custom quote?
Simply visit a caterer's profile and click "Request Quote." You'll be able to specify guest count, dietary preferences, and special requests that don't fit into standard packages.
